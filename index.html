Don't Panic!

Need to know how to do everything with our site? This is the place to look.

The WILL website is built for clarity. It uses design patterns or predictable blocks of content that appear consistently throughout the site. This makes it easier for users to find what they’re looking for. It should make your life easier as author, giving you freedom to create content without worrying about putting it in the right place at the right time. You should have creative freedom, and our users should have a great experience. Our promise is a great website for all concerned. This manual provides the details.
